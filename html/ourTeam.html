Our Team

Shahnaz Sultana


Shahnaz, an MTech in Thermal Engineering has considerable industry and teaching experience and is currently a visiting faculty with PES University and pursuing her PhD. Shahnaz has always believed that our children lack inspiration and vision to follow their dreams, and this prompted her to start Reading Stars India, to provide underprivileged children with books from all over the world and enable them to read, learn, enjoy and pursue their dreams.





Amthul Tabassum


Tabasuum brings almost three decades of corporate experience to Reading Stars India. A mechanical engineer by training, she worked with Bosch Ltd where she was all-India (aftermarket) Head of Packaging – Design and Development at Bosch Ltd. A driving desire to make a change among the underprivileged sections of society made her quit her corporate career and join hands with Shahnaz and Padma.









Dr. Padma Baliga


After teaching college and high school students for over two decades, Padma decided that she needed to focus on primary education and try and make a difference. A lifelong reader, she wants every child to experience the magic of books in their life. With a doctoral thesis in gender studies and Indian children’s literature and a Library Educator Certification from Parag, Padma joined hands with Shahnaz and Tabassum to set up classroom libraries in schools and help every child to have a reading life.
